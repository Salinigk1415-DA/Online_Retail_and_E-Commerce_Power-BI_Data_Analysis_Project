
##  ONLINE RETAIL & E-COMMERCE DATA

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing an online retail and e-commerce dataset to uncover key business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends. An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to visualize revenue distribution, customer segmentation, and purchasing patterns, enabling data-driven decision-making.
## Project Objectives
To analyze overall sales performance and identify top-performing products and categories

To understand customer demographics and purchasing behavior

To evaluate revenue distribution across different payment methods

To identify trends over time for better forecasting and planning

To provide actionable insights for improving sales and customer engagement
## Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional data from an online retail business, including:

Order Details: Order ID, Order Date

Product Information: Product Name, Category

Customer Data: Gender, Customer ID

Sales Metrics: Revenue, Quantity

Payment Information: Payment Method

The data represents multiple transactions across different products and customer segments.
## Data Preparation & Transformation
Cleaned missing and inconsistent values

Removed duplicates to ensure data accuracy

Standardized categorical fields (e.g., payment methods, product categories)

Created calculated columns and measures (e.g., Total Revenue, KPIs)

Transformed data using Power Query for better structure and usability

Established relationships between tables (if applicable)
## Tools and Technologies
Excel – Initial data cleaning and preprocessing

Power BI – Data modeling, visualization, and dashboard creation

Power Query – Data transformation and shaping

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs
## Analysis and Insights
Revenue by Product (Bar Chart)
Identified top-performing products contributing the highest revenue

Customer by Gender (Donut Chart)
Revealed customer distribution and potential target segments

Revenue by Payment Method (Column Chart)
Highlighted preferred payment options among customers

Revenue by Category (Funnel Chart)
Showed which product categories drive the most sales

Product by Category (Tree Map)
Visualized product distribution within categories

Revenue Trend Over Time (Line Chart)
Identified seasonal trends and sales patterns

KPI Cards
Displayed key metrics like Total Revenue, Total Orders, etc.
## Key Outcomes
Identified high-revenue products and categories for business focus

Recognized dominant customer segments for targeted marketing

Discovered preferred payment methods to optimize checkout experience

Observed sales trends to support forecasting and inventory planning
